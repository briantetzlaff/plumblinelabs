What Plumbline won't do

  • It won't show you a confident number your data can't support. Below the signal floor, it says so — and tells you exactly what would change that.
  • It won't guess. No probabilistic attribution at low order volume, where models overfit noise and produce a confident wrong answer.
  • It won't see everything. Server-side attribution misses some view-through and dark-social orders; they land in the unattributed bucket, labeled as exactly that.
  • It won't manage your ads. Plumbline decides the split between platforms; Advantage+ and PMax keep doing what they're good at inside each one.
  • It won't grow an enterprise tier. Brands spending $50k+/mo already have Measured, Rockerbox, and Compass. This is built for everyone they priced out.
