02

The problem

Ad platforms grade their own homework. Add up their dashboards and you get more sales than your store actually had. In a 2024 survey of US marketers, only about 1 in 5 said last click attribution reasonably reflects a platform's real long term impact, and 3 in 4 were moving away from it or wanted to. A 2025 survey of more than 700 app marketing leaders found only 18 percent trust their attribution data. Budgets get set on those numbers anyway.

Sources: EMARKETER and Snap, Media Measurement Survey, September 2024; Branch and GlobalSurveyz, 2025 State of App Growth Report.
