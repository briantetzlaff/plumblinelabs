04

What Plumbline won't do

  • It won't show a confident number your data can't support.
  • It won't guess. No modeled attribution at low order volume.
  • It won't pretend to see everything. Untracked orders are labeled, never hidden.
  • It won't rely on a tracking pixel that misses orders and breaks every time Apple or Google changes the rules.
  • It won't manage your ads. It decides the split; your platforms keep their channels.
  • It won't add enterprise complexity. Self-serve, no sales call, ever.
